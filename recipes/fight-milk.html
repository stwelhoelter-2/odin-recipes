Fight Milk

Description

Fight Milk is the pinacle of nutrition for every athlete across all sports. Loaded with crow protein, carbohydrates, and calcium, this drink lets you perform at your best.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of milk
  • 1 handle of vodka
  • 1 dozen crows eggs
  • 1 ground crow

Steps

  1. Mix milk and vodka
  2. Add 1 dozen eggs and ground crow to mix
  3. Blend for 30 seconds
  4. Enjoy!
