Five Course Survivor Feast

Description

A vast array of flavors await! There's no better way to learn about a local culture than to sample some of their favorite dishes and cuisine.

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 5 cups butod (bug larvae)
  • 2 pint cows' blood
  • 5 tarantulas
  • 5 balut (duck embryo)

Steps

  1. Pour the cows' blood into 4 shots of equal amounts.
  2. Put 1 cup of butod into a bowl; do for all 4 cups.
  3. Separately plate 1 tarantuala and 1 balut for each guest.
  4. Blend the remaining items together for a Survivor classic.
  5. Enjoy!
