GB Water Tonic

Description

Mmmm... look at that water. You know those geebs were milky. I know the water doesn't get changed often, but it's such as waste to just dump it after absorbing all that flavor. Instead, try a GB Water Tonic to feel your best when hydrating!

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 pint of GB water (minimum 50 geebs taken from it)
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bottle Canada Dry

Steps

  1. Mix GB water and Canada Dry together.
  2. Cut limes and squeeze into mix.
  3. Enjoy!
